Shift Stability System — Reliability by Design

Our three-layer model keeps care steady and on time, even when life happens.

How Coverage Works

  • Primary Caregiver — your consistent, familiar anchor.
  • Team Lead — provides check‑ins, transport if requested, and steps in immediately if the Primary is unavailable.
  • Care Supervisor — oversight and coaching with a 90‑day formal review cycle.

Late‑Arrival Protocol

  • We monitor real‑time clock‑ins for every shift.
  • If a caregiver is delayed, you receive an immediate ETA text or call.
  • Delay > 10 minutes? The Team Lead covers or dispatches a trained back‑up so the shift stays on track.
  • You always know who is coming and when.

FAQs

Do you really never miss shifts?
We are engineered for reliability. We have maintained 0 missed shifts over 14+ months by using layered coverage, real‑time monitoring, and proactive communication.
What if someone is running late?
You’ll receive an immediate ETA. If the delay exceeds 10 minutes, the Team Lead covers or dispatches a trained back‑up so your schedule remains intact.
Is 24/7 or live‑in available?
Yes. Typical cases are 56+ hours per week; our minimum is 20 hours/week to support stable staffing and guaranteed coverage.
Are caregivers employees?
Yes. All caregivers are W‑2 employees. We handle payroll taxes, Workers’ Comp, insurance, training, and supervision so you get consistent standards and accountability.
